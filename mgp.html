About us

Mastrovert Game Production (MGP) is a leading Game Developer and Publisher dedicated to shaping the gaming landscape in India.

Our mission is to foster innovation and talent within the Indian gaming industry, contributing to its growth and global recognition.

At MGP, we are committed to creating immersive gaming experiences that captivate audiences worldwide.

Our diverse portfolio spans across various genres, including online multiplayer, offline single-player, and hybrid games, catering to a wide range of gaming preferences.

With a focus on excellence and creativity, we strive to push the boundaries of game development, delivering high-quality products that showcase India's prowess in the gaming arena. Join us as we embark on an exciting journey to elevate the Indian gaming industry and make our nation proud
